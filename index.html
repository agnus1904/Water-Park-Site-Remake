About Sanjalika

A wonderfull water park

Based on millions of reviews on TripAdvisor, Sanjalika ranked #1 best water park in India and #12 in the World!! This beautiful waterpark has premium water rides, and over 50 attractions in total, including 9 Extreme & Thrill rides!!

The Main Festival area in the massive WAVE POOL is absolutely STUNNING!!
You can dance from day into the night to the beats of DJs K with hot dancers!!
